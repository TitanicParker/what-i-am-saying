The Periodic Table of Completed Intelligibility

The 54 ways anything can be realised

Understanding is so familiar that we rarely examine its completion. We notice when a proof closes, when a design works, when an argument is settled, when a pattern becomes visible, when a confusion gives way to clarity, yet we usually classify what has been understood rather than the act by which understanding has become complete. This system begins with a different question: not what is intelligible, but how intelligibility reaches a final landing. Its proposal is that every completed understanding can be recognised through three independent determinations. First, some kind of work has been completed. Second, that work has settled in a particular way. Third, something remains available after the settlement. The work is one of six forms: Distinction, Expansion, Relation, Correction, Integration or Rest. The settlement is one of three orientations: Toward, Across or Away. What remains is one of three survivor modes: Is, With or Does. Six kinds of work, three orientations and three survivor modes produce fifty-four coordinates, but the point is not arithmetic. The point is recognition. Anyone can recognise this; this, not that; this far; from here; held against; set right; one whole; arrived; nothing further is owed. These are not technical definitions imposed upon experience. They are familiar endings of understanding. The table gathers them into a single grammar and shows why each belongs where it does. The six work-families name what understanding accomplishes. Distinction makes something identifiable by establishing a difference. Expansion opens what can proceed, appear or become possible. Relation establishes a bearing by which one thing matters to another. Correction changes what cannot stand into something that can. Integration organises parts so that a whole exists or acts. Rest discharges the demand for further completion. These six are not stages that every inquiry must pass through in sequence, although they often form a lifecycle. They are six irreducible kinds of completed work. When we say this, we have distinguished. When we say from here, we have opened a source. When we say this bears on that, we have related. When we say set right, we have corrected. When we say one whole, we have integrated. When we say chosen, arrived or nothing further is owed, we have brought something to rest. A subject may be mathematical, practical, social, artistic or ordinary; the work can still be recognised by asking what changed in the understanding itself. The three orientations name how that work settles. Toward gathers the completion around a focus, source, pivot, adjustment, whole or selected end. Across lets the completion stand through a field, sequence, span, array, range of cases or distributed structure. Away completes by release from a previous governor: opposition, freedom, equivalence, a reset limit, equilibrium, transformation or discharge. Toward is not merely spatial nearness, Across is not merely extension, and Away is not merely rejection. They are three topologies of intelligibility. Toward gives a centre. Across gives a field. Away gives release. The same work changes character when its settlement changes. A distinction Toward can identify this; a distinction Across can locate something within this; a distinction Away can stand opposed. Expansion Toward begins from here; Expansion Across finds another way through; Expansion Away moves beyond. Relation Toward says this bears on that; Relation Across says then or each changes the other; Relation Away recognises the same arrangement or that one thing plays another role. The orientations are therefore not decorative row labels. They determine the form in which the work becomes complete. The three survivor modes name what remains once the work has settled. Is leaves the completed standing itself: this, from here, one whole, chosen. With leaves the bearing or condition by which the completion is sustained: this, not that; held against; held as one; settled if. Does leaves an accomplished operation or capacity: this far; more ways open; answered; together, able; arrived. Is does not mean a static object, With does not mean simple accompaniment, and Does does not mean arbitrary activity. They distinguish three modes of persistence. A completion may remain as what is now the case, as the relation that continues to hold it, or as what can now be done because the work has succeeded. Since these modes are independent of the work and of the orientation, each work-family must appear nine times: Toward/Is, Toward/With, Toward/Does, Across/Is, Across/With, Across/Does, Away/Is, Away/With and Away/Does. The table is the full crossing of these independent determinations. Distinction teaches the grammar most plainly because every intelligibility begins by making some difference hold. Distinction/Toward/Is is Focal Mark, whose final landing is this. The work is Distinction because something has become identifiable; the orientation is Toward because the completion gathers around one focus; the survivor is Is because what remains is simply the identified standing. Nothing further is needed. The shortest possible landing is this. Change only the survivor mode to With and the coordinate becomes Binary Bearing: this, not that. The same focal distinction now survives through its contrast. The identified thing is maintained together with what it excludes. Change only the survivor to Does and the coordinate becomes Boundary Act: this far. The distinction has become operative; it now governs passage, scope or application. These three coordinates show why Is, With and Does are not synonyms. Focal Mark leaves an identity, Binary Bearing leaves a contrastive bearing, Boundary Act leaves a working limit. Move Distinction from Toward to Across and the work no longer gathers around one focus; it distributes through a field. Across/Is is Scoped Standing: within this. Something is intelligible through the scope in which it stands. Across/With is Context-Bound Mark: this, here. The mark remains together with its position or context; remove the here and the this is no longer the same completion. Across/Does is Scoped Distinction: these among these. The distinction now operates throughout a plurality, sorting members within a field. Move Distinction to Away and completion is achieved by release from a governing inclusion or identity. Away/Is is Opposed Standing: opposed. Away/With is Graded Contrast: more and less, where distinction survives as degree rather than a binary cut. Away/Does is Exclusive Cut: not admitted, where release becomes an operative exclusion. The nine Distinction coordinates are not nine unrelated notions. They are what one work becomes when it settles toward a focus, across a field or away from a governor, and when it survives as a standing, a bearing or a capacity. Expansion keeps the same ninefold grammar but changes the work. Expansion does not ask what is this; it asks what is now possible. Expansion/Toward/Is is Source Field: from here. A source has become available. Toward/With is Source-Carried Divergence: from here, either way. The opening persists with alternatives carried by the same source. Toward/Does is Branching Output: more ways open. The source now produces possibilities. Across/Is is Alternate Passage: another way through. Expansion is distributed through a field as a newly available route. Across/With is Span-Carried Reach: reachable across. The opening persists through a span. Across/Does is Field Distribution: available throughout. Possibility has become an operative availability across the field. Away/Is is Released Option: free to choose. Expansion appears as release from a prior determination. Away/With is Continuity Bearing: still through. The release does not destroy continuity; a passage remains. Away/Does is Edge Opening: beyond. Expansion now operates past the former limit. Once Distinction has taught the grammar, these landings can be anticipated: from here, from here either way, more ways open; another way through, reachable across, available throughout; free to choose, still through, beyond. They are the nine invariant forms of opening. Relation changes the work again. What matters now is not identity or possibility but bearing. Relation/Toward/Is is Direct Bearing: this bears on that. A focal relation is simply established. Toward/With is Pivoted Balance: held against. The relation survives as a maintained bearing around a pivot. Toward/Does is Responsive Echo: answered. The relation has become capable of response. Across/Is is Sequential Link: then. One thing is intelligible through what follows another. Across/With is Reciprocal Shift: each changes the other. The relation is sustained across both sides, not owned by either alone. Across/Does is Relational Cycle: back into itself. The relation now operates recurrently. Away/Is is Pattern Alignment: the same arrangement. Relation survives release from the particular terms and becomes recognisable as structure. Away/With is Role Correspondence: this plays that role. The bearing persists through substitutable positions. Away/Does is Emergent Pattern: between us, something appears. The relation itself produces an outcome that neither side possesses alone. The family moves from direct bearing to sequence, reciprocity, structural alignment and emergence, but the underlying 3×3 grammar does not change. Correction is the work by which an insufficiency, error, imbalance or failure is made able to stand. Correction/Toward/Is is Focal Adjustment: set right. A particular point is corrected and remains corrected. Toward/With is Weighted Correction: rightly weighted. The adjustment persists through proportion, not mere replacement. Toward/Does is Load Transfer: the burden moves. Correction becomes operative by redistributing what had made the situation fail. Across/Is is Inversion Test: does it still hold reversed? Correction across a field requires a test of whether the claimed fitness survives reversal. Across/With is Dual-Bound Repair: right between both. The repair is sustained by the two sides it reconciles. Across/Does is Corrective Turn: turn where it fails. Correction becomes an ability to redirect at the point of breakdown. Away/Is is Limit Reset: a better limit. The fault is released by changing the boundary that governed it. Away/With is Conditioned Reversal: reverse when this holds. The correction survives as a condition under which direction changes. Away/Does is Disorder Release: free of the fault. Correction is complete as an operative freedom from the previous disorder. Set right, rightly weighted, the burden moves; does it still hold reversed, right between both, turn where it fails; a better limit, reverse when this holds, free of the fault: the family is recognisable because each landing is the familiar completion of repair under a distinct coordinate. Integration is not correction. Parts can be correct and still fail to form a whole. Integration performs the work by which plurality becomes organised unity. Integration/Toward/Is is Focal Whole: one whole. The many are apprehended as one. Toward/With is Frame-Bound Whole: held as one. The unity persists through the frame or relation that contains it. Toward/Does is Joint Capacity: together, able. The whole can now do what the separate parts could not. Across/Is is Role Array: each in its place. Integration is distributed as ordered position throughout the field. Across/With is Span-Bound Interlock: held together across. The whole persists through connections spanning its parts. Across/Does is Role Activation: in operation. The organised array functions. Away/Is is Released Equilibrium: able to remain. Integration has become stable beyond the conditions of assembly. Away/With is Sustained Equilibrium: tension that holds. The unity persists not by eliminating difference but by sustaining it in balance. Away/Does is Convergent Whole: many become one act. The integrated plurality now performs as a single accomplishment. One whole, held as one, together able; each in its place, held together across, in operation; able to remain, tension that holds, many become one act. By now the reader should be deriving more than receiving. The coordinates are increasingly recognisable before their names are read. Rest is the work by which the demand for further completion is discharged. It is not inactivity. It is the positive achievement of sufficiency. Rest/Toward/Is is Settled Alternative: chosen. A focal possibility has been resolved into one standing outcome. Toward/With is Settled Unity-Bearing: together now. The selected completion persists with what has been gathered into it. Toward/Does is Path Completion: arrived. The course has accomplished its destination. Across/Is is Settled Recurrence: it returns. Completion stands across repetition as something that can come again without losing itself. Across/With is Condition-Bound Settlement: settled if. The rest persists with the condition that sustains it. Across/Does is Case Settlement: settled across the cases. Completion operates throughout the relevant field. Away/Is is Transformation Release: still itself. What has changed is free of the transformation yet remains itself. Away/With is Retrospective Settlement: now it makes sense. The completion persists through a new relation to what came before. Away/Does is Continuation Discharge: nothing further is owed. The demand for completion no longer governs what follows. Chosen, together now, arrived; it returns, settled if, settled across the cases; still itself, now it makes sense, nothing further is owed. These are not nine decorative endings placed under Rest. They are the nine forms in which sufficiency can settle and survive. The table can now be read without treating its fifty-four cells as fifty-four independent definitions. Begin with the column and ask what work has completed: has understanding distinguished, expanded, related, corrected, integrated or rested? Then take the row in two parts. Ask how the completion settled: Toward a focus, Across a field or Away from a governor? Ask what survived: Is, With or Does? The answers determine the coordinate. Distinction/Toward/Is cannot land anywhere but this because focal identification surviving as a standing is precisely this. Expansion/Away/Does cannot land anywhere but beyond because released possibility surviving as capacity is an opening past the edge. Relation/Across/With cannot land anywhere but each changes the other because a distributed relation surviving as a bearing is reciprocity. Correction/Toward/Does becomes the burden moves because focal repair surviving as an operation transfers what had prevented the fit. Integration/Away/With becomes tension that holds because released unity surviving as a relation is sustained equilibrium. Rest/Away/Does becomes nothing further is owed because completion released from further governance and surviving as an accomplishment is discharge itself. The names in the cells are therefore useful but secondary. Focal Mark, Source Field, Direct Bearing, Focal Adjustment, Focal Whole and Settled Alternative are compact names for the six Toward/Is coordinates; they are not the foundations of the system. The foundations are the axes. A name can be reconsidered while the coordinate remains determinate, because the coordinate is generated by three constraints. The final landings are especially important because they return the abstract grammar to recognisable experience. This. From here. This bears on that. Set right. One whole. Chosen. A person need not accept a theory to understand those completions. The system begins from that ordinary recognition, then reveals the invariant structure within it. The claim is consequently stronger than the claim that the table is useful. It proposes that completed intelligibility has an architecture. Every completion does some work, settles somewhere in the topology of understanding, and leaves something available. If the work-families are exhaustive, if Toward, Across and Away exhaust the forms of settlement, and if Is, With and Does exhaust the modes of survival, then the fifty-four coordinates are not an assortment but a closed field. Removing one would leave a genuine combination unnamed. Adding another would require a seventh kind of work, a fourth orientation or a fourth survivor mode, and would therefore have to show a form of completion not already expressible by the grammar. The burden of the system is not carried by the elegance of six times three times three. It is carried by whether a reader can recognise the landings, derive why they occupy their positions and use the axes to identify completed understanding in unfamiliar cases. The recognisability of the system does not depend on finding an example that uses the exact words printed in a cell. A landing is invariant because many different situations can realise the same completed form. A child pointing to an object, a scientist isolating a variable and a court identifying the matter at issue may all complete Focal Mark even though their subjects, methods and vocabularies differ. A map boundary, a safety threshold and a rule of admissibility may realise different Distinction coordinates because one identifies this far while another establishes not admitted. Likewise, a new route through a city, an alternative proof and a workaround in software may all realise Alternate Passage: another way through. The table is therefore not a catalogue of phrases to match literally. Its phrases are minimal recognitions that expose the structure of a completion. To use the table well, one must distinguish the topic from the intelligible work being done. Two statements about the same topic may occupy different coordinates, while statements from remote disciplines may share one coordinate. The question is never merely what words were spoken. It is what completion those words achieved. This is also why the coordinates can be tested. Hold two axes constant and change the third. If the meaning does not change in the expected way, either the coordinate has been misunderstood or the proposed landing is wrong. Moving from Is to With should change a standing into the bearing that sustains it; moving from With to Does should change that bearing into an accomplished capacity. Moving from Toward to Across should distribute the settlement from a centre through a field; moving from Across to Away should reveal the completion as release from a former governor. Moving between columns should change the kind of work while preserving the row grammar. The table thus provides not only names but internal checks. Its coherence is experienced through controlled variation: one axis changes, the others remain, and the landing changes by necessity rather than association. That is the meeting point between the essay and the table. The essay does not replace the table, and the table does not replace the essay. The essay lets the reader watch the grammar become recognisable; the table compresses the full grammar into one surface. A reader may stop after Distinction and already possess the key: work, orientation, survivor. Another may continue through Expansion and Relation until the repetition becomes predictive. Another may read all six families and test every landing. However far the reader proceeds, the same operation is available: identify the work, identify the settlement, identify what remains. The table can then be referred to not as an authority but as a coordinate map. Its purpose is not to tell the reader what to think. Its purpose is to make visible the invariant forms through which anything can become completely intelligible.
